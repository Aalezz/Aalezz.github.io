Who I am

About Me

I'm Alezz Aldumaini, an Artificial Intelligence Engineer and the CEO & Founder of Suraya AI, based in Tabuk, Saudi Arabia. I design, build, and deploy full-stack AI systems — from backend infrastructure and AI pipelines to production-ready web platforms.

My work spans the full AI stack: I built Lumiq, an end-to-end LLM-powered educational platform with RAG, multi-role dashboards, and AI tutoring; MentorX, a personalized learning assistant with adaptive study plans and multimodal summarization; a fine-tuned CodeT5 + LoRA model for automated code debugging; a Voice AI Agent with wake word detection, real-time STT, emotional TTS, and persistent memory; and a Multi-Agent Research System where specialized AI agents collaborate to produce research reports.

I've also developed AI systems for vehicle damage detection, aspect-based sentiment analysis using RoBERTa, and dental disease diagnosis combining YOLOv8 and EfficientNetV2 with Grad-CAM explainability.

As a founder, I don't just imagine the future of AI — I build it. I'm driven by a clear mission: to engineer intelligent systems that solve real problems at scale.
